7 easy eco laundry tips (that save money too)

  • Choose biodegradable detergents and skip optical brighteners where possible.
  • Wash with cold water; modern detergents clean effectively at low temps.
  • Run full loads and select eco/quick cycles to reduce water and energy use.
  • Pre‑treat stains to avoid re‑washing and overusing bleach or harsh chemicals.
  • Use HE washers and dryers with moisture sensors to prevent over‑drying.
  • Air‑dry or hang dry whenever possible—great for delicates and denim.
  • Pick a local wash & fold Abu Dhabi plan with scheduled pickup & delivery to cut extra trips.
