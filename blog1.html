How to Care for Your Clothes

Proper garment care extends the life of your clothes and keeps them looking fresh. In this detailed guide, we cover everything from sorting laundry to choosing the right detergents, and tips for stain removal. Learn how to wash, dry, and store your favorite pieces for maximum longevity. For more laundry tips, visit SpinXpress Blog.

Key Tips:

  • Sort clothes by color and fabric type.
  • Use gentle detergents for delicates.
  • Remove stains promptly with the right solution.
  • Air dry when possible to prevent shrinkage.
  • Store garments in a cool, dry place.
