Key Tips

  • Sort clothes by color and fabric type (cotton, synthetics, delicates).
  • Use gentle, eco‑friendly detergents for delicates and baby clothes.
  • Treat stains immediately with the correct solution (protein, oil, tannin).
  • Air dry when possible to reduce shrinkage and save energy.
  • Store garments clean, in a cool dry place; use breathable covers for suits.

FAQs

What is the best way to remove oil stains?
Apply a small amount of liquid dish soap, let it sit 10–15 minutes, then wash warm (check care label).
How often should I dry clean suits?
Every 3–4 wears unless visibly soiled; use breathable garment bags for storage.
