Sort smart: color, fabric, and care labels

Separate lights, darks, towels, and delicates. Check labels for dry clean only and hand‑wash symbols—great candidates for dry cleaning in Abu Dhabi. Use mesh bags for lingerie and items with hooks or zippers.

Stain removal 101

Treat stains quickly: blot, don’t rub. For oil, use dish soap; for protein stains (food, sweat), use an enzyme remover. For delicate outfits like kanduras and abayas, consider green dry cleaning Abu Dhabi to avoid color bleed or shrinkage.

Washing settings: eco & cold‑water

Modern detergents clean well in cold water. Choose eco or quick cycles and run full loads to save water and energy. If you’re busy, our wash & fold service can handle weekly basics for you.

Drying: air‑dry vs machine

Air‑dry whenever possible to reduce wear. If using a dryer, select low heat and remove garments promptly. Heat can set stains and cause shrinkage—especially in the UAE’s warm climate.

Ironing & steaming

Use the correct temperature for fabric types and plenty of steam for cotton and linen. For crisp office wear, book our ironing service in Abu Dhabi with pickup & delivery.

Storage in Abu Dhabi’s climate

Humidity can encourage odors and mildew. Store clothes in a cool, dry wardrobe. Use breathable garment bags for formal wear and avoid plastic covers for long‑term storage.
